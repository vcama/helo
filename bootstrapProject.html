About Us

Worldwide Education Awareness is an organization who strives to inform the public about the countries who don't receive the right education or where students have to put their lives in danger in order to receive some education. Many of these students don't finish high school or even enter high school. Worldwide Education Awareness wants to increase the percentage of graduating high school students in those countries with poor education and hopefully increase the percentage of students being able to go to college. Our goal as an organization is to become an asset to these communities in help and involve others to inform themselves and feel motivated to help, and add themselves to the movement of helping hands.
